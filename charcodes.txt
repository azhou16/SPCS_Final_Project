Snake char codes

Head:

W = up
S = down
A = left
D = right

Body:

T = up
G = down
F = left
H = right

Tail:

I = up
J = down
L = left
K = right

Other:

X = food

Z = ground










Snake char codes

Head:

0 = W = up
1 = S = down
2 = A = left
3 = D = right

Body:

4 = T = up
5 = G = down
6 = F = left
7 = H = right

Tail:

8 = I = up
9 = J = down
10 = L = left
11 = K = right

Other:

12 = X = food

13 = Z = ground
